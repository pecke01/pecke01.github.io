mgranstrom.com

My name is Marcus Granström and I am a software engineer working for EPiServer. I work mostly with distributed stuff including ElasticSearch, Cassandra and also do some work with node.js.

I am one of the co-founders of 200OK AB that built the search service Truffler based on ElasticSearch. 200OK was acquired by EPiServer in May 2012 and the product rebranded as EPiServer Find. My co-founders were, Joel Abrahamsson and Henrik Lindström.

You can find me on twitter as @pecke01.
